Services

The dermatologists of Medicare Clinic, PA treat patients of all ages. We use the latest technology and treatment protocols to diagnose and treat diseases and conditions of the skin, hair, and nails.

General Dermatology

  • Skin cancers (basal cell carcinoma, squamous cell carcinoma and melanoma)

  • Pre-cancerous lesions (actinic keratoses)

  • Moles and other pigmented lesions (brown spots)

  • Eczema (atopic dermatitis)

  • Acne

  • Psoriasis

  • Seborrheic keratoses

  • Fungal and bacterial infections

  • Herpes simplex (fever blisters and cold sores)

  • Warts

  • Allergic reactions and hives

  • Hair loss

  • Nail disorders and infections

  • Blistering diseases

  • Other rare, benign and malignant skin disorders
